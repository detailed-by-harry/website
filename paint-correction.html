Why have paint correction?

Over the years you vehicle is subject to not only the elements, but also the natural abrasion of the paint while the car is in use. Over time you may not notice, but having a paint correction treatment will restore your vehicle to it's factory finish. By that we mean that the car will find its shine and the paint finish will have much more depth.
